Gluten-Free Club Sandwich Recipe

Description

Sometimes you need a super quick meal to fill up your screaming stomach. Satisfy your hunger with this club sandwich! Follow along on the original website Flippin-delicious.

Ingredients

  • 3 slices Canyon Bakehouse Heritage Style Gluten Free Bread
  • 1 tablespoon Mayo
  • 2 slices smoked turkey deli meat
  • 2 slices bacon, cooked
  • 2 slices black forest ham deli meat
  • handful spring mix or other lettuce/greens
  • 2 slices swiss cheese

Steps

  1. Lightly toast the bread.
  2. Lightly spread one side of the toasted bread with mayo and top with turkey and bacon. Top it with another piece of bread with mayo spread on BOTH sides.
  3. Layer spring mix, ham, and swiss followed by another piece of bread spread with mayo.
  4. Cut in half on the diagonal and take a big bite.
  5. Enjoy and Share!
